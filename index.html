How to Make a Vision Board

Assembly time: 30 minutes
Awesome rating: 100% awesome

Are you feeling burnt out? Apathetic? Depressed? You're not alone. These feelings are common among students everywhere. One way to refocus and reset is by creating a vision board. It’s a fun, visual way to clarify your goals, dreams, and personal inspiration—helping you stay motivated year-round.

  • Things You'll Need

  • A planner or notebook
  • Art supplies
  • Yoga mat and dumbbells
  • A phone or iPod
  • A book

  1. Plan out your day with a planner

    Planning your day in advance helps build a consistent routine, which can make your goals clearer and reduce the chances of burnout.

  2. Art supplies

    Grab some markers, glue, scissors, and magazine cutouts. Make your board colorful and meaningful—it’s your personal inspiration map! Think about your short- and long-term goals, and let your creativity guide you.

  3. Yoga mat and dumbbells

    Moving your body can instantly lift your spirits. Take a break from screens and do a short yoga or strength session—it’s great for your mental health and motivation.

  4. Phone or iPod

    Use your device to create playlists that energize and motivate you. Whether it’s classical for studying or upbeat tracks for workouts, music aligned with your goals can improve focus and boost your mood.

  5. A book

    Reading can be a powerful tool for personal growth. Pick up a book that inspires you, teaches something new, or helps shift your mindset. Whether it’s a biography, self-help, or fiction with a strong message, the right book can recharge your energy and give your mind a positive focus.

Stay inspired, stay strong.
